Scallop Stir-Fry Noodles

Masterchef USA Season 3 winner, Christine Ha's Scallop Stir-Fry noodles

Stir-fry noodles in a wok

Ingredients

Noodles

  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons rice vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 8 ounces dried rice vermicelli, soaked in cold water for 30 minutes
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 3 shallots, diced
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon peeled, finely chopped fresh ginger
  • 1 pound oyster mushroom
  • 2 carrots, peeled and julienned
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced

Scallops

  • 16 large sea scallops
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons chili oil
  • Spring fresh cilantro

Instructions

Servings: 4

Noodles

  1. Whisk fish sauce, soy sauce, chicken stock, lime juice, mirin, vinegar, and sugar in a small bow until sugar dissolves. Set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil over high heat. Prepare a large bowl of ice water. Drain the soaking water from the vermicelli. Add vermicelli to the boiling water and cook for just 10 second. Quickly drain the noodles and place them in the ice water to cool completely. Drain well.
  3. Heat a large wok over medium-high heat. Add the canola oil, then stir in the shallots, garlic, and ginger. Cook until fragrant and the shallots begin to soften, about 1 minute. Add the mushrooms and carrots and stir-fry until they soften slightly, about 5 minutes. Stir in the vermicelli and the reserved sauce and stir-fry to coat, about 30 seconds. Stir in the scallions.

Scallops

  1. Season the scallops with salt.
  2. Heat a large saute pan over high heat. Add the canola and chili oils and swirl to coat the pan, then add the scallops and cook until dark brown, about 2 minutes per side.

To serve, divide the vermicelli among 4 wide bowls. Top with scallops, garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.
